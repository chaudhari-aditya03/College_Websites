About Our School

Cultivating Minds, Building Futures, Shaping Communities.

Our Philosophy

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence in Sangamner KH and beyond, fostering a dynamic learning environment that inspires students to achieve their full potential, embrace lifelong learning, and contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and balanced education that empowers every student with critical knowledge, essential skills, and strong ethical values. We strive to create a nurturing community where curiosity is ignited, creativity flourishes, and every individual feels valued and supported.

Our Journey

Established in **[Year of Establishment - e.g., 1985]**, Amruteshwar Secondary and Higher Secondary School has been a cornerstone of education in Sangamner KH for decades.

Founded by the **Sahyadri Bahujan Vidya Prasarak Samaj**, our institution began with a vision to provide quality education to the local community, promoting academic excellence and holistic development. Over the years, we have grown from a small school into a comprehensive educational hub, continually adapting to the evolving needs of our students and society.

We take immense pride in our rich heritage, the thousands of successful alumni who have passed through our gates, and our unwavering commitment to shaping future leaders and responsible citizens.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in teaching and learning, encouraging intellectual curiosity and critical thinking.

Integrity & Ethics

Upholding honesty, respect, and strong moral principles in all interactions and endeavors.

Community & Collaboration

Fostering a sense of belonging, teamwork, and mutual support among students, staff, and parents.

Innovation & Adaptability

Embracing new ideas and methodologies to prepare students for a rapidly changing world.

Holistic Development

Nurturing intellectual, emotional, social, physical, and spiritual growth in every child.
