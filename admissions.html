Admission Information

Eligibility Criteria

  • Secondary School (5th to 10th): Completion of previous grade with passing marks.
  • Higher Secondary (11th & 12th):
    • Science: Minimum 60% in 10th grade (with Science & Math preferred)
    • Commerce: Minimum 50% in 10th grade
    • Arts: Open to all streams with a passing 10th certificate

For detailed information on eligibility or specific requirements, please contact our admissions office.
